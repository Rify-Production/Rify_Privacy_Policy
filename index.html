This Privacy Policy explains how RIFY PTY LTD ("Rify", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Rify mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When using Rify, you may provide:

  • Camera-captured photos
  • Text content, such as public service announcements (PSAs)
  • Account-related information, including authentication identifiers

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information to operate and improve the App:

  • Precise location data (GPS), if permission is granted
  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version) and app usage metrics (e.g., interactions, timestamps)

2. Camera and Photos

Rify only permits photo uploads captured directly via the device camera. The App does not access your personal photo library.

3. Location Data

Location data is used to provide nearby, location-based content and functionality. Access to location is required for core App features.

We do not sell or rent your location data to third parties.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Provide location-based features
  • Display user-generated content
  • Enhance performance, security, and user experience
  • Ensure safety, prevent misuse, and moderate content

4.1 Advertising

The App may display advertisements via third-party providers such as Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). All ads are non-personalised and are not targeted using personal data or user behavior outside the App. Ads may use contextual information or limited device data (e.g., device type or general location) to display relevant content.

No advertising identifiers are collected without consent, and users under 16 are not targeted.

4.2 App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Rify does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising purposes. No tracking consent is required to use non-personalised ads in this App.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers (e.g., Firebase) to operate the App, store and synchronize data, analyze usage, and improve performance
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect rights, property, safety, or security, including that of Rify or other users

6. Service Providers

We engage third-party service providers, including Firebase, to store data, provide analytics, and support App functionality. Such providers may collect analytics or crash reporting data, but this is used solely to maintain, improve, and secure the App.

7. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored using secure third-party services. While we implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, no system is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to operate the App or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account or content, subject to technical and legal limitations.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. Users under 16 are not targeted with personalised advertisements.
